Privacy Policy

Last updated: [December 26, 2025]

Fatemi Calendar is a simple calendar calculator. It runs locally on your device and is designed to work without collecting any personal data.

In short: We do not collect, store, share, or sell any user data.

What the App Does

  • Shows Fatemi calendar dates and conversions.
  • Does not let you create events, add personal notes, or sync with calendars.
  • Works like a calculator: you use it, and that’s it.

Data We Collect

None. We do not collect any data from you or your device.

  • No sign in, no sign up, no accounts.
  • No analytics, no tracking, no advertising.
  • No third-party SDKs.
  • No server storage (the app does not send your data anywhere).

Third-Party Services

The app is built using Apple’s iOS frameworks (Swift / Apple APIs). We do not use third-party libraries or third-party tracking tools.

Your Rights

  • You can stop using the app at any time.
  • You can delete the app at any time to remove it from your device.
  • If we ever change how the app works in a way that affects privacy, we will update this policy and update the “Last updated” date.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes, we will update this page and the date at the top. Any changes apply from the date shown.
